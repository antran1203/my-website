TOILET CULTURE

Dec, 2023

Art Direction

Toilet Culture is an article being designed followed by MOLD magazine style, which has the components of dynamic layout, vibrant color, and being experimental. In relation to that, the layout was designed followed by the theme taken from the article content idea relating to the combination between Eastern and Western culture, as well as contemporary and modern aesthetic.
