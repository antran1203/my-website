GIA - Lunar New Year Advent Calendar

"GIA," a Sino-Vietnamese term for home and family, embodies the heart of Vietnamese culture. As the namesake of our Tet gift box, it symbolizes unity and reunion, echoing the tradition of gifting during Tet. For those far from home, "GIA" is a reminder of cultural beauty through festive dishes. "GIA" transcends mere materiality; it serves as a conduit bridging generations. For adults, it evokes cherished memories, while for children, it extends an invitation to embrace timeless values. Ultimately, "GIA" is a testament to the enduring bond that defines Vietnamese families across time and space.

About the box

The box is modeled after a traditional Vietnamese tea cabinet, complete with four compartments, central sliding doors, and open cabinets on each side. Tea cabinets, staples in Vietnamese homes, typically adorn living rooms where families gather, especially during Tet. By echoing this familiar symbol, the design evokes a sense of homecoming and recreates a cherished space for family gatherings during the Tet holiday.

The 12 zodiacs

The central theme of the box centers on the 12 zodiac animals, each symbolizing a different generation corresponding to an age. The cover story depicts the preparations for the New Year's party of these animals, alongside images of traditional Vietnamese Tet snacks. The illustration on the box cover mirrors a familial Tet celebration, where generations come together to either prepare for Tet or indulge in the delightful dishes enclosed within.

Fruit tags

Behind each tag is product information, along with New Year wishes based on the meaning of traditional Tet snacks
